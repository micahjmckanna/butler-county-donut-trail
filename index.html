Try them all on the Butler County Donut Trail!

Explore, devour and conquer your way through the Butler County Donut Trail! All you need to complete this sweet road-trip is your passport and an empty stomach. Venture to all 13 of our local Donut Shops, get your passport stamped at each stop, and then redeem your passport for your 2020 Donut Trail T-Shirt.

Planning Your Donut Trail Trip?

Give the Donut Trail concierge a call! A Donut Trail expert can help customize your experience. Simply call 513-860-0917 for assistance with finding somewhere to stay, planning your route, and finding fun must-dos during your Donut Trail Getaway.

Concierge hours are Monday - Friday between 8:30AM-5:00PM.
