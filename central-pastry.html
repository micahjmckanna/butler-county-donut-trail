Central Pastry Shop

  • 1518 Central Avenue
    Middletown, OH 45044

  • (513) 423-4431

  • Mon–Sat 6:30 am – 5:30 pm

Central Pastry Shop is a full retail bakery featuring donuts, cakes, cookies, and a variety of specialty breads. Did you know Central Pastry Shop is one of the stops on the popular Butler County Donut Trail? Fluffy cinnamon squares, the famous "Uglies", and seasonal Paczki are just a few of the treats you can dream of at this Middletown shop. Be sure to stop and take a photo with their Donut Trail mural, and place a pin of your hometown on their Donut Trail map.
