Holtman’s Donut Shop

  • 9558 Civic Centre Blvd.
    West Chester OH 45069

  • (513) 755-1261

  • Sunday & Monday: 6:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Holtman's Donut Shop is located at the Streets of West Chester, and is one of the stops on the Butler County Donut Trail! Holtman’s bold and beautiful donuts are perfect for Instagram. Enjoy donuts piled high with cereal, bacon, or even made from scratch marshmallow on their S’mores. This shop provides a sneak peek into the donut making world with windows looking into the prep and decorating space.
